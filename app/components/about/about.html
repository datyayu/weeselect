About

weeselect is a MEAN stack app created just for the purpose of doing it. Also because just clicking random on champ select wasn't fun at all.

Champion's builds are currently based on the list from champion.gg posted by Zenitholt on reddit. However, the plan is to have them updated automagically from champion.gg on a regular basis... once I get motivation for getting the parsing part done (eh...).

If you find a bug or want to help improve the site, you can do it on github or tell me on twitter or my email.
